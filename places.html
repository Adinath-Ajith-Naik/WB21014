MANANCHIRA SQAURE

Manchiria is the heart of the city, which cites important institutions like town hall and the public library. One of Kozhikode's oldest buildings, the common wealth trust office is situated here. The large pond and the park are well known landmarks. The musical fountain inside the maidan and meticulously landscaped lawns surrounded by traditional Kerala style architectural buildings are a fascination for visitors.


KOZHIKODE BEACH

Kozhikode will always have a special place in the history of Kerala as it is here that Vasco-da-Gama first landed and the legendarySpice Route came into existence. This region still has a historic charm to it. Kozhikode Beach is the personification of the numerous characteristics that make Kozhikode so unique. A lot of street vendors selling hot hot dishes.


Thamarassery Churam

Thamarassey Churam the road conenction to Wayanad, hill station. A beautiful scenery is available from the top of Thamraserry Churam. It is situated 62km from Kozhikode City. While travelling through Thamarassery Churam the misty peaks glare back towards you thereby making you fall in love with it.


HiLite Business Park and Mall

HiLite Mall and Business Park is the largest mall in Kozhikode city. It is a place where you are able to get all the things that you require. HiLite Mall has 2 phases thereby making it huge with a lot of fun filled activities to do inside. With the business park adjacent to it, makes it easy to access and a proper place to keep all your official meeting. Snow Fantasy and Go Kart are the main attractions of HiLite Mall.
