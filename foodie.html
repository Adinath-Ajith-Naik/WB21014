Foodie Corner

Kozhikode known as the "LAND OF SPICES". Like the title says come and fill up your tummy and hearts.


Hotel Rahmath

Rahmath hotel is one of the oldest restauarnts that has been situated in the heart of the city. The aroma of the Biriyani hits the atmosphere of the city as soon as the huge vessels are opened. The Mutton Biriyani and Chicken Biriyani is very much famous. Huge queue could be seen infront of the hotels during the lunch time.


Hotel Paragon

Paragon is itself a brand name with a lot of franchises all over the world. It has a wide variety of dishes. It is a must visit hotel where you get to eat all the different types of dishes prepared by expert chefs. The sub branches of Paragon are named as "Salkara" which also serve the same quality items at a very moderate price.


Adaminte Chayakada

Adaminte Chayakada is situated in the Kozhikode Beach where all people gather to spend time with their family and friends. Adam's provide a wide variety of evening snacks along with evening tea which makes it the perfect place to start your day at the beach. They also have a wide variety of other dishes along with other grand combos. During the Holy Month of Ramdan they provide special eateries which is a must tried food.


Amma Mess House

Ambika Mess House the centre of attraction of the city for having a great lunch which will fill both your stomach and hearts. Famous for its various types of fish savouries. Just check out any food vlogger on YouTube and you are sure that you will find a vlog done by them. There would be a huge rush to have lunch here because it erases your hunger without ripping up your pockets.
